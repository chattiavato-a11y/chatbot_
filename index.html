About Us

Human-centered, secure, and intelligent by design.

Chattia is a cloud-native experience studio focused on building future-ready, data-driven, and emotionally intelligent digital products. We blend AI orchestration, DevSecOps automation, and modern UX neuro-design to deliver compliant, resilient, and delightful customer journeys.

Our Mission

Unify intelligent automation with human-centered design so teams can ship faster, stay compliant, and build experiences that feel effortless on any device.

What We Build

We craft adaptive interfaces, secure cloud workflows, and AI-powered assistants that scale across e-commerce, support, and operations without sacrificing trust or usability.

Security & Compliance

Our OPS CyberSec Core maps NIST CSF, CISA Cyber Essentials, and PCI DSS requirements into a full Identify → Protect → Detect → Respond → Recover lifecycle.

AI & Data Intelligence

We integrate privacy-aware AI/ML and telemetry to optimize performance, personalize journeys, and continuously improve reliability through measurable insights.

Experience Design

Our UX/UI systems follow 2025+ HCI trends, WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility, and Core Web Vitals to create inclusive, engaging, and responsive digital experiences.

Governance & Trust

We establish shared governance across IT, security, and legal with clear documentation, version control, and policy visibility for confident decision-making.
