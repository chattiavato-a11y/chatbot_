About Chattia for OPS

Human-centered, secure-by-design support assistant.

Mission

Deliver fast, safe, multilingual assistance that respects user privacy while staying aligned to regulatory guardrails.

  • Accessible UX built around WCAG 2.1 AA guidance and responsive layouts.
  • Security-first defaults: CSP, Turnstile, input normalization, and consent-aware preferences.
  • Continuous improvement from telemetry signals while minimizing data collected.

What powers the experience

  • Cloud-native delivery with edge security controls and origin allowlists.
  • Voice input/output readiness through the Web Speech API.
  • Localization toggles for English and Spanish with persistent settings after consent.
  • Modular assets that separate theming (backgrounds and tokens) from UI logic.

How to engage

  • Use the chat for quick answers and operational support.
  • Open the consent and T&C modals from the chat footer for full context.
  • Submit security or privacy questions through the Contact page.
