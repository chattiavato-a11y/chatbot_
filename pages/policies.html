Privacy, Consent, and Terms

Unified reference for end users and operators.

Privacy

Chattia and OPS do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information (PII). Avoid sharing sensitive data in prompts.

  • Chats may be logged for reliability, abuse prevention, and quality improvements.
  • Input sanitization, CSP, and Turnstile verification help reduce script injection and spam.
  • Data minimization: only chat text, language preference, and security telemetry are processed.
  • Compliance alignment: NIST CSF, CISA Cyber Essentials, and PCI DSS safeguards guide storage and handling.

Terms & Conditions

  • Use the assistant respectfully: no harassment, illegal content, or attempts to exploit the service.
  • Availability is best-effort; responses may be incorrect. Verify critical guidance with a human expert.
  • Security checks (e.g., Cloudflare Turnstile) and rate limits may block abusive or automated traffic.
  • Content you submit must not include malware, embedded scripts, or copyrighted material you do not own.

Consent & Controls

  • Consent is required to enable chat. Denying consent disables input until you re-accept.
  • Language and theme preferences are stored only when consent is granted; otherwise they remain in-memory.
  • For removal or incident requests, contact the OPS team through the Contact page.
